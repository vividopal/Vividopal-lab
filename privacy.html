Privacy & Data Sovereignty

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

1. Data Sovereignty Protocol

Vivid Opal Lab LLC ("the Company") collects data essential for managing digital creative assets. We adhere to a strict Data Sovereignty Protocol, ensuring that all production data is processed within hardware-isolated environments to prevent identity leakage.

2. Hardware-Isolated Security

To safeguard intellectual property, all operations are conducted through Isolated Nodes. We implement AES-256 encryption and SSL/TLS protocols to ensure that digital footprints remain non-traceable across third-party networks.

3. Information Minimization

The Company enforces a "Zero-Knowledge" policy regarding creator metadata. All assets undergo rigorous sanitization to remove geolocation and hardware identifiers before deployment to public-facing platforms.
