Privacy & Data Sovereignty

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

1. Data Sovereignty & Collection

VividOpal Lab LLC ("the Company") collects data essential for the management and optimization of digital creative assets. This includes encrypted media files, anonymized performance metrics, and compliance verification data. We adhere to a strict Data Sovereignty Protocol, ensuring that no unencrypted personal identification data is stored beyond the mandatory verification period.

2. Production Environment Security

To safeguard intellectual property and creator identity, all asset management operations are conducted within Hardware-Isolated Production Environments. We implement AES-256 at-rest encryption and SSL/TLS in-transit protocols to prevent unauthorized cross-platform data mapping or identity leakage.

3. Digital Footprint Sanitization

The Company enforces a "Zero-Knowledge" metadata policy. All creative assets undergo a rigorous scrubbing process to remove geolocation and hardware identifiers before distribution to any third-party subscription-based or digital content platforms.

4. Limitation of Liability

The Company acts as a technical service provider for digital identity management. Under no circumstances shall VividOpal Lab LLC be held liable for third-party platform actions, including account suspensions, or for any indirect damages such as loss of professional certifications or reputational risks incurred by the client.
