Effective Date: March 22, 2026

1. Data Sovereignty & Collection

VividOpal Lab LLC ("the Company") collects data essential for managing and optimizing digital creative assets. We adhere to a strict Data Sovereignty Protocol, ensuring that no unencrypted personal identification data is stored beyond the mandatory verification period.

2. Production Environment Security

To safeguard intellectual property, all asset management operations are conducted within Hardware-Isolated Production Environments. We implement AES-256 at-rest encryption and SSL/TLS in-transit protocols to prevent identity leakage.

3. Digital Footprint Sanitization

The Company enforces a "Zero-Knowledge" metadata policy. All creative assets undergo a rigorous scrubbing process to remove geolocation and hardware identifiers before distribution to any third-party platforms.
